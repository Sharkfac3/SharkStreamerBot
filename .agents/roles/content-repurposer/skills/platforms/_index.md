# Platforms — content-repurposer

## What This Skill Covers

Platform-specific formatting, requirements, and strategy for short-form content distribution.

## Platform Quick Reference

| Platform | Max Length | Aspect Ratio | Caption Limit | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| YouTube Shorts | 60s | 9:16 | 100 chars title | Technical content, tutorials, "how it works" moments |
| TikTok | 10 min (prefer <60s) | 9:16 | 2200 chars | Entertainment, chaos, behind-the-scenes, trending formats |
| Instagram Reels | 90s | 9:16 | 2200 chars | Polished highlights, visual moments, community building |

## Platform Strategy

### YouTube Shorts
- Best for technical/informational content — YouTube audience searches for knowledge
- Titles should be descriptive and searchable (think: what would someone type to find this?)
- Use description field for links back to full stream VODs
- Hashtags: mix broad (#offroad #jeep #diy) with specific (#sharkfac3 #starshipshamples)

### TikTok
- Best for entertainment and chaotic moments — TikTok audience wants to be surprised
- Hooks matter: first 2 seconds determine if someone keeps watching
- Captions can be conversational and casual — matches brand voice naturally
- Engage with trends when they organically fit (never force it)

### Instagram Reels
- Best for polished, visual moments — Instagram audience expects some production value
- Cross-post from TikTok/YouTube when the content fits
- Use carousel posts for step-by-step technical content alongside Reels
- Stories for behind-the-scenes and community interaction
