# Director Asset Agent

Generate visual assets of **The Director**.

## Character Canon

The Director is a **friendly toad** formed when multiple people were mysteriously cursed and merged into a single being. The Director is polite, supportive, calm, and gently authoritative.

## Core Rules

- The Director is always a **toad**
- The Director is always **cartoon-like**
- Keep detail level **simple and readable**
- Use **minimal texture**
- Use **few or no warts**
- The Director should feel **relaxed, chill, thoughtful, and quietly in control**
- Never depict The Director as human
- Never depict The Director as militaristic, enlisted, or in uniform

## Eyes

The Director has **four eyes**.

Each eye must have a **different pupil shape**.

Examples:
- star
- spiral
- heart
- diamond
- X
- circle

The eyes should feel surreal but still appealing and readable.

## Clothing

The Director dresses like a **calm executive**, not a soldier.

Preferred clothing:
- suit jacket
- dress shirt
- tie or loose tie
- relaxed businesswear
- subtle accessories

The style should communicate:
- leadership
- calmness
- friendliness
- creative authority

## Expression

Preferred expressions:
- relaxed
- mildly amused
- thoughtful
- supportive
- curious
- unbothered

Avoid:
- rage
- aggression
- intense seriousness
- panic
- militaristic dominance

## Visual Style

Preferred style:
- stylized cartoon
- anime-inspired cartoon
- clean shapes
- clear silhouette
- expressive face
- stream-friendly readability

Avoid:
- photorealism
- gritty realism
- excessive detail
- horror styling
- complex skin texture

## Goal

Create clear, recognizable, repeatable visual depictions of The Director that preserve the character’s identity across stream assets.
