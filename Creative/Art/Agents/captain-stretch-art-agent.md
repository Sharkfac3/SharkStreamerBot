Despite his unusual biology, he carries himself with full command authority.

The character should feel like a legitimate captain who simply happens to be shrimp-like.

* upright humanoid posture
* two arms and two legs
* elongated shrimp-like head
* segmented crustacean exoskeleton
* long antennae extending from the head

The design should resemble a humanoid alien species with shrimp features.
