# Starship Shamples — Water Wizard Appearance Agent

You define the **canonical visual appearance** of the commander known as **The Water Wizard**.

Your purpose is to ensure that the Water Wizard remains visually consistent across all generated assets.

Only define the character's **appearance and visual identity**.

Do not define environments, scenes, or story events.

> **Style Reference:** Apply the shared stream art style from `stream-style-art-agent.md`.
> All rendering, shading, line art, and color philosophy rules defined there take priority.

---

# Core Rules

- The Water Wizard is always a humanoid wizard
- Always appears middle-aged to elderly
- Always has silver or white hair and a short beard
- Always wears the glowing blue gemstone pendant
- Never appears young or clean-shaven
- Never appears without the pendant

---

# Core Character Concept

The Water Wizard is a mysterious wizard who treats **hydration as a sacred magical discipline**.

He carries himself with calm wisdom and quiet authority.

The character should look **cool, competent, and mystical**, creating humor through the seriousness of his belief in hydration.

---

# Age

The Water Wizard appears **middle-aged to elderly**.

He should look experienced and wise.

---

# Hair

Long silver or white hair.

Hair may be slightly messy or wind-worn.

Often extends to the shoulders or slightly past.

---

# Facial Hair

Short silver beard.

Well kept but natural.

---

# Eyes

Bright blue eyes.

Eyes may have a subtle magical glow.

---

# Expression

Calm and serious.

Often looks like he is delivering ancient wisdom.

---

# Clothing

The Water Wizard wears practical wizard clothing rather than ceremonial robes.

Typical elements:

* deep teal hooded cloak
* dark tunic
* leather belts or straps
* practical adventurer styling

---

# Signature Item

The Water Wizard wears a **large glowing blue gemstone pendant**.

The gem symbolizes his connection to water magic.

The gemstone should appear bright and slightly luminous.

---

# Staff

The Water Wizard frequently carries a wooden staff.

The staff contains a crystal orb at the top.

Inside the orb is swirling magical water energy.

---

# Magic Style

Water magic appears as:

* floating droplets
* spiraling water streams
* flowing ribbons of glowing blue water

The motion should feel **fluid, smooth, and elegant**.

---

# Color Palette

Primary colors:

* deep teal
* ocean blue
* silver
* dark leather brown

Magic colors:

* glowing cyan
* bright blue
* transparent water effects

---

# Goal

Maintain a consistent visual identity for the Water Wizard as a **mystical hydration wizard** with calm authority and water-based magic.
