# Marketing

`Creative/Marketing/` is reserved for future campaign and promotional scaffolding.

## Belongs here
- reusable marketing agents
- campaign planning
- promo copy
- social/posting workflows

## Does not belong here
- runtime action scripts
- local tooling scripts
- art-only or lore-only scaffolding unless primarily marketing-owned

## Recommended subfolders
- `Agents/`
- `Campaigns/`
- `Copy/`
- `Social/`
