# Marketing

`Creative/Marketing/` contains campaign scaffolding, promotional copy, and social content for the SharkStreamerBot / Starship Shamples brand.

## Before creating anything here

Always read the brand foundation documents first:

| Document | Why |
|---|---|
| `Creative/Brand/BRAND-IDENTITY.md` | Vision, mission, values, and the neurodivergent metaphor — everything produced here should reflect this |
| `Creative/Brand/BRAND-VOICE.md` | Tone and language conventions for each context (stream titles, community posts, social clips) |

Load the `brand-steward` skill when generating any marketing output.

## Belongs here

- Clip strategy and highlight planning
- Stream title and description templates
- Social post frameworks and copy
- Campaign or event planning
- Promotional agent prompts

## Does not belong here

- Runtime action scripts (→ `Actions/`)
- Local tooling (→ `Tools/`)
- Art generation without marketing context (→ `Creative/Art/`)
- Lore or story content (→ `Creative/WorldBuilding/`)

## Recommended subfolders

- `Agents/` — reusable marketing agent prompts
- `Campaigns/` — specific campaign plans
- `Copy/` — reusable copy templates (titles, descriptions, announcements)
- `Social/` — clip strategy, social post frameworks, highlight guides

## Brand voice reminder

Marketing for this brand is never:
- Hype-driven ("INSANE," "YOU WON'T BELIEVE")
- Aspirational or luxury-positioned
- Generic ("join us for a fun stream!")

Marketing for this brand is always:
- Specific to what is actually happening
- Honest about failure and chaos
- Written with the assumption that the audience wants to participate, not just watch
